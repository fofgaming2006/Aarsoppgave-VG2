Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



How can I check the weather for another city?

You can easily check the weather for another city by entering the city name and country in the search field and clicking the search button.

In which temperature unit is the weather displayed?

The weather is displayed in Celsius (°C).

Where does the app retrieve the weather data from?

The app retrieves the weather data from the OpenWeatherMap API.

How accurate is the weather information?

The weather information is as accurate as the data we receive from the OpenWeatherMap API. However, local conditions and weather variations may affect accuracy.

How is the weather information updated?

The weather information is updated automatically when you search for a new city.

How does the app store my data?

The app does not store personal data. The weather data displayed is only retrieved from the OpenWeatherMap API to provide you with the most accurate information about weather conditions.

Do any of the links at the bottom of the website work?

Unfortunately, some links do not have access to the locations. If you click on them, you may be sent to the same place. However, the social media links will send you to the correct places anyway.

Can I receive weather alerts?

Unfortunately, the app currently does not offer a feature to send weather alerts. However, we are exploring the possibility of adding this functionality in future updates.
